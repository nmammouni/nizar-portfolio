Power BI Insights: Driving Strategic Change through Data-Driven Dashboards

1. Mecklenburg County Input Dashboard

Shaping the Future of Our Community: This dynamic platform revolutionizes community engagement by offering residents a window into local park and recreation projects. With active park enhancements and greenway trail expansions at the forefront, the dashboard empowers local leaders to make data-backed decisions. By harnessing diverse datasets and building visually compelling Power BI dashboards, I support strategic planning, resource allocation, and customer satisfaction, ensuring the county’s parks evolve in alignment with community needs.

2. Operational Dashboards with DAX

Data-Centric Decision-Making for Leaders: I created a series of advanced dashboards aimed at optimizing operational efficiency for department heads. By defining key business requirements and KPIs, I developed a robust data model, utilized DAX measures to calculate actionable metrics, and delivered visualizations that enable leaders to streamline their operations, enhance budget planning, and build strategic roadmaps. This project not only offers insightful data but also drives a shift toward a data-centric culture in the organization.

Setup and Data Transformation

For both projects, I leveraged Power BI’s advanced data transformation tools. This included cleansing data, merging multiple sources, creating new calculated fields using DAX, and shaping the datasets to fit the specific requirements of the visualizations. These transformations ensured that the data was accurate, reliable, and actionable.

Conclusion

These Power BI dashboards demonstrate my ability to work with large datasets and deliver clear, actionable insights to decision-makers. By creating dashboards that are interactive and visually engaging, I help organizations make informed decisions that lead to strategic change and operational efficiency. Whether it's shaping community infrastructure or optimizing internal operations, my work with Power BI drives meaningful impact.
