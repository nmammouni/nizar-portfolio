Spotify Data Analysis 2023

Unlocking the Soundtrack of the Future: This project dives deep into Spotify's 2023 data, revealing trends that shape the future of music consumption. Bangalore Restaurant Insights with Zomato Data

Serving Success on a Platter: By analyzing Zomato data, we transformed raw numbers into actionable insights, helping our client craft a competitive edge in Bangalore's bustling restaurant scene.

With machine learning at the core, we unearthed what drives customer satisfaction, guiding the client in optimizing their entry strategy to meet the city's culinary demands.
