Computer Vision Breakthroughs: Harnessing AI for Early Detection

Melanoma Detection Project

Saving Lives through Early Detection: This project utilizes cutting-edge computer vision techniques to assist in the early detection of melanoma from dermatological images. By preprocessing and augmenting image data, we applied convolutional neural networks (CNNs) for precise feature extraction.

We trained a classification model capable of identifying melanoma with high accuracy. This project showcases the power of AI in the medical field, offering a tool that could significantly enhance early diagnosis and potentially save lives.
